WHAT IS
START - UP 1.0

Start-up 1.0 is an Ideathon Series and an Analytical Championship organized by the Professional Development Avenue of the Rotaract Club of University of Colombo School of Computing. Start-up 1.0 serves as a platform encouraging schoolers and university undergraduates to think beyond the here and now and make their innovative ideas a reality.

Theme of START - UP 1.0

Decent work and economic growth | Promote sustainable industrialization and foster innovation | Responsible consumption and production.
